The wheel\_data dataframe contains monthly information on overall amounts of trash collected at specific dumpsters in Inner Harbor in Baltimore. It also includes quantities of individual items found in the trash, such as plastic bottles, grocery bags, and cigarette buds. It contains 344observations. In 2017, the median number of sports balls found in a dumpster was 8.

The overall\_precip\_data dataframe contains monthly information on quantities (in inches) of precipitation in Inner Harbor in Baltimore. It contains 344observations. Based on the available data, the total precipitation in 2018 was 23.5.
